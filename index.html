Design thinking and workflow.

When working on projects I applied strong understanding of the design thinking process to ensure the workflow focused on user needs. The process involves deconstructing the brief from the client, empathise with the users involved, research and define problems, then ideate and collaborate within a team. Following this after multiple brainstorms and analysis of the research collected, wireframes and prototyping is conducted.
