Design thinking and workflow.

During my studies each project required a strong understanding of the design thinking process to ensure the workflow focused on user needs. It was first necessary to emphathise with the users involved, research and define problems then ideate and collaborate within a team. Following this after multiple brainstorms and analysis of the research collected, prototyping was then conducted.

"This is NOT a linear process and multiple prototypes and tests need to be conducted to address the needs of the users. Once testing and prototyping is complete the next cycle is implementated."
