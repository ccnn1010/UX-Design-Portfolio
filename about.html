About.

I am a highly motivated and creative professional with a passion for technology and visual design. I am fascinated about user experience and my background in education gives me an edge on how to empathise and relate to how people interact with technology.

Experience.

Previously I was an educator with over 10 years of experience who has worked in several institutions in adult education and secondary schools. My main teaching areas were Visual Arts, Mathematics, Science and English. My keen interest in technology lead me to study a Graduate Certificate in Interaction Design at the University of Queensland Australia in 2019 and to further enhance my skills in 2020 I studied a five-month Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Wild Code School Madrid. I decided to do the bootcamp as I believe to be a great UX/UI designer I think it is important to have the technological knowledge to work with developers and understand the web development process.

I love learning new things, travelling and expressing myself creatively. In the past I was a visual artist and have skills in painting, illustration and digital design. In fact all of the digital illustrations in this portfolio were created by me using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
