About.

I am a highly motivated and creative professional with a passion for technology and visual design. To take this passion to the next level I studied Interaction Design at the University of Queensland Australia and Web Development at Wild Code School Madrid. I am fascinated about user experience and my background in education gives me an edge on how to empathise and relate to how humans interact with technology.

I love learning new things, travelling and expressing myself creatively. In the past I was a visual artist and have skills in painting, illustration and digital design. I also have a keen interest in health, sustainability and education.

Feel free to contact me. I’m looking forward to working together and discussing new ideas.
